About

The idea behind this website is to help track how certain events or public gatherings may have affected the spread of Covid-19 in a certain area. According to WHO it takes on average 5-6 days for symptoms to develop, but it can take up to 14 days. When you search for a date the website will generate a result which highlights five, ten and fifteen days after the date in order to account for the delay of when a gathering takes place and cases will start being reported. For now, the service is only available for US locations, and results will be narrowed down to a county level.
